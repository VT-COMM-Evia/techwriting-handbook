Writing References

Instructors may want to suggest students use an optional online or print-based reference to help with basic concepts of writing, grammar, and punctuation.

In determining the requirements for a writing reference, you should remember that the students of this course are not English or writing majors and therefore have different needs than those of an English or writing major. This means using a book that is both inexpensive and easy to follow for a non-writer. You should consider making a published writing reference optional, and point students toward free websites with which you are familiar and comfortable.
