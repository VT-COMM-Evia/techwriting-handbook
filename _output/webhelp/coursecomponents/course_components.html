Course Components

Course components are the topic lectures, assignments, quizzes, and other resources that comprise the complete virtual classroom for this course.

  • Lesson Modules
    A module provides a mechanism for you to organize and deliver lessons, including all of the lectures, assignments, and other components related to each lesson.
  • Textbooks and References
    Because this course is conducted in a virtual environment, textbooks and related references are a particularly useful aid in teaching this course.
  • Course Calendar
    Because students work on their own schedule in an asynchronous, online course such as this one, it is important that they have an accurate calendar so they are constantly aware of upcoming deadlines.
  • Announcements
    Announcements allow you to remain in contact with your students and provide them with timely updates regarding class activities and issues.
  • Tests and Quizzes
    While most work in this course will be through writing papers, tests and quizzes serve as useful supplements to ensure students are keeping up with conceptual material.
  • Lectures
    A lecture is the method through which instructors teach specific concepts of a given topic. When delivered through an online course, lectures are synonymous with tutorials.
  • Exercises
    Exercises are low-risk deliverables that allow students to learn the concepts and practice the craft of technical writing.
  • Assignments
    This handbook refers to papers, projects, and workshops that students submit for a grade as assignments .
  • Discussions
    Discussions provide a forum through which students can ask and answer questions, share information, and express thoughts and opinions.
