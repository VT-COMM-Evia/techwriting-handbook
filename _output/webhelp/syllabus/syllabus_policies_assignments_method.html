Format and Method for Submission

The syllabus should explain the required file formats for assignments and policies on how to take tests and quizzes.

The relevant information for policies on the format and method of assignment submissions could be included as a subsection to the assignments subsection (in the policies section) and includes:
  • A statement that explains your policies on how to take tests and quizzes
  • A statement that explains your policies for file submissions

Example

Format and Method for Submission

You will take all quizzes and tests through Canvas. You must submit all projects, papers, and exercises as a PDF document through Canvas unless otherwise directed in class.
