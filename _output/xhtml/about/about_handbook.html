About This Handbook

This handbook provides graduate teaching assistants (GTAs) and other instructors who are new to teaching the online version (virtual campus) of ENGL 3764: Technical Writing guidelines for teaching the course. Experienced instructors might also find the content useful to help ensure they are meeting the English department's objectives for the course.

  • Overview of Content
    This handbook provides guidelines for teaching the online version (virtual campus) of ENGL 3764: Technical Writing and includes specific examples you can use or modify to meet the needs of your specific classes.
