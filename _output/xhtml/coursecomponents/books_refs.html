Textbooks and References

Because this course is conducted in a virtual environment, textbooks and related references are a particularly useful aid in teaching this course.

  • Technical Writing Textbooks
    This course requires, at a minimum, a textbook that explains the concepts and genres of technical writing.
  • Writing References
    Instructors may want to suggest students use an optional online or print-based reference to help with basic concepts of writing, grammar, and punctuation.
