Exercises

Exercises are low-risk deliverables that allow students to learn the concepts and practice the craft of technical writing.

You can post exercise descriptions through the assignments feature in Canvas. However, there are different tools through which you can implement them. For example, you could have students work on exercises through Google Docs and then work with them either asynchronously or in real-time to help them hone in on specific areas for improvement. Such a process allows students to become more skillful before working on graded assignments.
