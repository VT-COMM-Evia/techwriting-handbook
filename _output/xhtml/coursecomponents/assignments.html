Assignments

This handbook refers to papers, projects, and workshops that students submit for a grade as assignments .

You can post assignment descriptions through the assignments feature in Canvas. As with exercises, there are different tools through which you can implement them. For example, you could interact with students through Google Docs to help them improve their work. However, keep in mind that assignments should measure a student's success with the corresponding material and thus be performed independently by the student.
