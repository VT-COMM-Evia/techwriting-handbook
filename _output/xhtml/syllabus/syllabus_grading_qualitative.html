Qualitative Grading

Because written papers are qualitative in nature, some instructors prefer to grade them based on a letter grade (as opposed to a quantitative number). If you want to have content that explains how qualitative grading may work with regard to written deliverables, you can include such a section.

You could include the following information as a subsection to the grading section to explain quantitative grading as it may apply to graded deliverables:
  • A statement that qualitative grading is applied to written work
  • A matrix that maps letter grades to numeric grades
