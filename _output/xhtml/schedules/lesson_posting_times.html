Lesson-Posting Times

While you are free to post lessons on any day and time you find appropriate for your class, keep in mind that students in an online course could be located in different time zones around the world, especially during summer and winter sessions when students return home.

Because of the potential variation of time zones among students, lesson-posting times are an important consideration, particularly for deadlines. Timeframes that work for students in the continental United States are 1:00 or 2:00 PM ET, which keep lesson postings and deadlines not too early for students in earlier time zones and not too late for students in later time zones.
