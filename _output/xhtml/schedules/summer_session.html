Summer Session

There are two summer sessions, each lasting approximately six weeks.

Summer sessions require lessons to be posted multiple times per week. This means using a predictable pattern for the student, such as every Tuesday and Thursday or every Monday, Wednesday, and Friday.

The Monday, Wednesday, and Friday model works well for summer sessions because it allows for skipping a lesson on summer holidays such as Memorial Day without risking falling behind in the class.

Refer to the summer session quick reference to see a recommended model for rolling out lessons during a summer session.
